Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Dot and Cross Technology offer?

We specialize in Web Development, Digital Marketing, SEO, and E-Commerce Solutions. Whether you need a stunning website, effective marketing strategies, or SEO optimization to boost your search rankings, we’ve got you covered!

How long does it take to develop a website?

The timeline depends on the complexity of the project. A basic website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while advanced e-commerce or custom web apps can take 6-12 weeks. We ensure timely delivery without compromising quality.

Can you improve my website’s Google ranking with SEO?

Yes! Our SEO experts use on-page and off-page SEO techniques, keyword research, content optimization, and backlink strategies to improve your search rankings and increase organic traffic. Results typically show within 3-6 months.

What makes your digital marketing services stand out?

We create data-driven and customized marketing strategies, including social media marketing, PPC campaigns, content marketing, and email marketing, to help you reach your target audience and boost conversions.

Do you provide post-launch support and maintenance?

Absolutely! We offer ongoing support, maintenance, and updates to ensure your website or online store runs smoothly, remains secure, and stays up to date with the latest trends and technologies.
